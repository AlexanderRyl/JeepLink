Preserving Culture,
Upgrading Efficiency.

The Jeepney is more than a vehicle; it's a symbol of Filipino ingenuity. But the old system is struggling to keep up with modern demands.

JeepLink bridges this gap. We provide the digital infrastructure—GPS, automated payroll, and ridership analytics—that allows traditional cooperatives to compete with modern taxi services and ride-hailing apps, all while keeping the iconic Jeepney on the road.

  • LPTRP Compliant Tracking
  • Automated Cooperative Reports
  • Driver Safety Monitoring
